Clemson University Graduate Program in Chemistry

Explore world-class graduate programs with excellent funding opportunities.

Apply Now

Funding Opportunities

  • Teaching Assistantships: Provide a stipend and tuition waiver in exchange for teaching undergraduate laboratory courses.
  • Research Assistantships: Offer stipends and tuition waivers for working on research projects with faculty members.
  • Fellowships: Competitive fellowships are available from various sources, including the university and external organizations.

Application Process

Submit your application through the Clemson University Graduate School. Required materials include:

  • Official transcripts
  • GRE General Test scores (recommended, not required)
  • At least two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement
  • Resume/CV
  • TOEFL/IELTS/PTE Academic/Duolingo (for international students)

Prerequisites

  • Bachelor's degree in chemistry or a related field
  • Strong background in general, analytical, physical, inorganic, and organic chemistry
  • Research experience is highly desirable
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0

Important Dates

Application Deadline: December 1st

English Language Proficiency for International Students

  • TOEFL: Minimum score of 80 (speaking score of 20 recommended)
  • IELTS: Minimum overall score of 6.5 (speaking score of 8.0 recommended)
  • PTE Academic: Minimum score of 54 (speaking score of 74 recommended)
  • Duolingo: Minimum score of 115 (speaking score of 130 recommended)

For More Information

Please visit the Clemson University Graduate School website.

To apply, visit the Chemistry Department Graduate Application page.
