Louisiana State University (LSU)

Assistantships and Fellowships

The Department of Entomology at Louisiana State University (LSU) offers various assistantships and fellowships to support graduate students. These opportunities provide financial assistance and valuable research and teaching experience.

Vice Presidential Agricultural Development Graduate Assistantship:

This prestigious new assistantship averages around $26,000/yr plus tuition remission. It is given to a student with exceptional credentials. Applicants are required to have a Bachelor’s (minimum 3.0 GPA) or Master’s (minimum 3.5 GPA) degree, and a minimum GRE score of 300.

Departmental Graduate Assistantships:

The department has several research-based assistantships for qualified applicants. These assistantships are competitive.

Departmental Teaching Assistantships:

The department has several teaching-based assistantships for qualified applicants. These assistantships are competitive, with preference given to Ph.D. candidates.

Grant-funded Graduate Assistantships:

Faculty within the department working on grant-funded projects might have assistantships available through those grants. It is recommended that you contact faculty within the department to determine what is available.

LSU Fellowships:

Numerous funding opportunities exist at LSU. Click here for a list of current fellowships.

Funding from National Organizations:

National fellowship opportunities can be found at the grants.gov webpage.

Departmental Awards and Scholarships:

The L.D. Newsom Graduate Student Award in Entomology: This award is given to individuals who exhibit excellence in academic achievement and graduate research in the Department of Entomology at Louisiana State University. One award is available for nominees in the M.S. program and one award for a Ph.D. candidate each year. The amount of each award is $1,000. Deadline for application is March 1 of each year.

The Boethel Scholarship Graduate Student Award in Entomology:

This award is given to a full-time M.S. or Ph.D. student who exhibits excellence in academic achievement and has made satisfactory progress on his/her graduate degree program in the Department of Entomology at Louisiana State University. Preference is given to students with research in the area of integrated pest management. One award is available for a nominee in the M.S. or Ph.D. program each year. The amount of the award is $1,000. Deadline for application is July 1 of each year.

The Roussel Scholarship Graduate Student Award in Entomology:

This award is given to a full-time M.S. or Ph.D. student who exhibits excellence in academic achievement and has made satisfactory progress on his/her graduate degree program in the Department of Entomology at Louisiana State University. Overall GPA must be 3.4 or greater. One award is available for a nominee in the M.S. or Ph.D. program each year. The amount of the award is $1,000. Deadline for application is July 1 of each year.
