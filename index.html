Our Services, Tailored to Your Needs

Prime One-On-One Consultation

Experience personalized one-on-one consultation with our CEO, Associate Professor A.Q. Overseas Admission Application

We offer personalized guidance and support for overseas admission and scholarships, catering to graduates of all levels. By signing up, you'll gain access to updated templates, 3 hours of direct mentorship, and ongoing email support for crafting your SOP, CV, and cold emails, as well as guidance on research areas, scholarships, and countries.

Right Through Program (RTP)

We provide comprehensive support for busy/determined students through our Right Through Program (RTP), including personalized application assistance, 24-hour consultation, and pre/post-departure guidance for studying in developed nations. This program is suitable for First-class, Second-class Upper and Lower Division degree holders.

Career Coaching Program

Our Career Coaching Program, led by Associate Professor A.Q. Adeleke, offers skill mapping, career path selections, resume enhancement, job search strategies, LinkedIn optimization, interview preparation, and salary negotiation techniques to empower you for success.

Educational Research and Data Analysis

We have a skilled research team that can handle diverse projects for clients from various sectors, such as education, technology, healthcare, and social sciences. Additionally, we have a successful history of securing grants for clients by offering comprehensive grant proposal services. These services include identifying funding sources, creating project budgets, crafting proposal narratives, and submitting applications, all tailored to the client's project goals.

USA Permanent Resident EB2NIW

We provide you with information on accelerated processing times compared to other employment-based categories, eliminating the need for a permanent job offer. Additionally, learn about the benefits associated with our services, such as work authorization, green card eligibility, and the potential for obtaining citizenship.

Students Visa Application Support

We simplify student visa applications, offering personalized assistance from experts who can navigate visa complexities and ensure your application meets destination-specific requirements for a higher chance of success.
